Le Programme d'Études
The 2-Month Intensive Curriculum

Strategically designed to build confidence first (b chwiya b chwiya), then push to advanced expression — just like how Darija flows naturally into new ideas.

📅 Weeks 1 – 3
Phase 1: Foundation
التأسيس
🎯 Core Vocabulary & Simple Structures

Getting kids comfortable speaking without fear. We start with simple phrases so they can quickly express basic needs and ideas — no pressure, just progress.

  • Core vocabulary acquisition
  • Simple sentence structures
  • Speaking without fear of mistakes
  • Daily expressions bridging Darija → English
►
📅 Weeks 4 – 8
Phase 2: Expression
التعبير المعمق
🎯 Complex Phrases & Connectors

From short answers to full impressive paragraphs! Kids learn to connect thoughts logically and express complete, confident ideas.

  • Connectors & complex sentence building
  • Storytelling & paragraph expression
  • Public speaking & mini presentations
  • Fun challenges & gamified activities 🎮
